# Blinkit Sales Analysis: Power BI & SQL Project

## ⚡ Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's (formerly Grofers) sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution. By processing a dataset of 8,523 items, the study identifies key drivers of revenue and outlet efficiency to help optimize quick-commerce operations.

## 📊 Key Insights & Business KPIs
* **Total Sales Performance:** Analyzed a total revenue of **$1.20M** with an average customer rating of **3.9**.
* **Consumer Health Trends:** Discovered that **Low Fat** products are the primary revenue drivers, contributing **$776.32K** to total sales.
* **Geographic Dominance:** Identified **Tier 3 locations** as the highest revenue-generating regions, accounting for **37.21%** of total sales.
* **Outlet Optimization:** Determined that **Medium-sized outlets** outperform Small and High-sized categories in overall sales volume.
* **Top Categories:** Isolated **Fruits, Vegetables, and Snack Foods** as the leading item types in terms of transaction frequency and revenue.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit
* **SQL Server:** * Performed **Data Cleaning** by standardizing inconsistent tags (e.g., converting 'LF' and 'low fat' to 'Low Fat').
    * Wrote advanced queries using **Window Functions** to calculate sales percentage contributions across various outlet sizes.
* **Power BI:** * Built an interactive dashboard with dynamic filtering by Outlet Location, Size, and Item Type.
    * Developed custom measures for **Average Sales, Total Items, and Average Ratings**.
* **Data Modeling:** Established a robust schema to analyze the correlation between outlet establishment year and sales growth.

## 🖥️ Dashboard Features
* **KPI Scorecard:** Real-time tracking of Total Sales, Avg Sales, and Item Count.
* **Fat Content Analysis:** Comparative charts showing sales distribution between Low Fat and Regular products.
* **Outlet Performance Matrix:** A breakdown of metrics (Revenue, Ratings, and Volume) by Outlet Type and Size.
* **Establishment Growth Trend:** Visualization of sales performance relative to the age of the outlet.

## 📂 How to Run
1. Run the `Blinkit Analysis SQL.docx` queries in **SQL Server** to clean and prepare the data.
2. Open the **Power BI** file and connect it to your data source.
3. Use the slicers to explore specific trends across different outlet tiers and item categories.
